History

Lucky Man Concerts was formed in 2001 and promotes over 150 concerts per year in Arizona and New Mexico. The Marquee Theatre is located 1 mile north of Arizona State University in Tempe, AZ.

In 2017, Lucky Man Concerts and The Marquee Theatre celebrate their 14th Anniversary. Past events at the Marquee Theatre include Prince, Adele, Katy Perry, Nine Inch Nails, and Muse to name a few.
